## Mathematical Workspace/Grapher
"I like how the Desmos graphing calculator has a bunch of built-in evaluation options on the sidebar, but I want more of a Mathematical Workspace (trademark pending), like essentially a digital notebook where you can also have it evalute expressions."
- themonkifier, December 2023

# General Calculator
Allows:
- +, -, *, /, %, and ^
- trig and inverse trig
- hyperbolic and inverse hyperbolic trig
- sqrt, cbrt, exp, ln, and other such functions
- lexical substitution of common constants: e, pi, c, h, hbar, R, k

note: modulus (%) of two complex numbers doesn't always give a result that agrees with WolframAlpha or Google, but they don't always agree with each other either. I think my result is right, but I don't need to die on this hill either

# Can do matrix math
Allows:
- multiplication by scalar
- matrix addition, subtraction, and multiplication
- computing inverse
